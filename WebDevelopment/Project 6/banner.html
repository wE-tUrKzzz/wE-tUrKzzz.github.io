Devil May Cry 5

Devil May Cry 5 is a 2019 action-adventure game developed and published by Capcom. The game is the sixth installment overall and the fifth mainline installment in the Devil May Cry series. The plot follows returning protagonists Nero and Dante as they are hired by a mysterious stranger named V to stop the Demon King Urizen. Players control Nero, Dante, and V, who each feature a different playstyle.

Characters

Dante: The Legendary Devil Hunter

 Dante is a paranormal mercenary, private investigator and vigilante Devil Hunter dedicated to exterminating evil demons and other malevolent supernatural forces; a mission he follows in pursuit of those that killed his mother and corrupted his brother.
Nero: The Young Devil Hunter

 Nero is raised in Fortuna and served as a Holy Knight in the Order of the Sword, a religious group that worships Sparda and fights to protect the world from demons.
V: The Mysterious Poet

 A human with demonic ties, V presents himself as Dante's new client to put him on the trail and takes Nero as backup insurance.
Vergil: The Dark Slayer

 Vergil is the older twin brother of Dante in contrast with his younger brother's embrace of humanity and initial rejection of the demonic. Stoic and reserved, Vergil displays a willingness to do anything in his quest to obtain the power of his father, Sparda.
