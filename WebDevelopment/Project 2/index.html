EXPLORE

  • Burnham Park: Enjoy a serene stroll, paddle boating on the lake, or simply relax in the gardens of this beautiful park.
  • Session Road: The main commercial street of Baguio, lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes.
  • The Mansion: A stunning landmark, the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines.

CULTURE

  • Panagbenga Festival: Experience the vibrant flower festival that showcases the rich cultural heritage of Baguio through colorful floats and street dancing.
  • Handloom Weaving: Visit local weaving centers to see the intricate craft of weaving traditional textiles, a vital part of Baguio's cultural identity.
  • Baguio Public Market: Explore the market for unique handicrafts and local produce, offering a glimpse into the daily life and culture of the Baguio people.
Learn More

IMMERSE

  • Camp John Hay: Explore the lush pine trees and scenic trails, perfect for hiking, biking, and enjoying the fresh mountain air.
  • Botanical Garden: Discover a variety of plant species and vibrant flowers in this well-maintained garden, ideal for leisurely walks and photography.
  • La Trinidad Strawberry Farm: Experience the joy of strawberry picking and enjoy the scenic views of the farms and mountains.
